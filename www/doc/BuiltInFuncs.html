Class: BuiltInFuncs

BuiltInFuncs

new BuiltInFuncs()

BuiltinFuncs is a container for built in functions, mostly taking {ItemCon} or {CatCon} object references as arguments. These contrast with the general Library functions which operate purely on values. In general these functions may appear in dREL methods though in simpler form - with implied arguments, or wrapped arguments.
